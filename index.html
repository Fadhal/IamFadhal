About me

I am a self-made Front-End/UI Developer & Designer with great passion in design and development of Websites. It is my love of Web that steered me away from my Dubai based eight-year long career in Tourism, E-Commerce and Photography and made me break into Web.

Through years I have gained practical expertise in major Front-End technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and design tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator along with PSD2HTML techniques.

My designs are very simple, yet trendy...minimal, yet precise. I’m a constant learner, and I like keeping my skills updated and tools as polished and shiny as they come.

As someone who had been brought up in a culturally diverse city of Dubai, I speak (& write) fluent English, Hindi, Tamil and Arabic - particularly valuable if you are looking to target visitors from the still-young and un-penetrated middle-eastern markets for your products and services.
